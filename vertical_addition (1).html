Vertical Addition Adventure

Welcome to the Vertical Addition Adventure!

How to play:

  • Add three numbers shown vertically.
  • Type your answer and press Enter or Submit.
  • Or use voice: say "What's the addition of [number] [number] [number]".
  • Answer within the time limit to earn points!
  • Complete 10 questions to level up. Higher levels, bigger numbers!
  • Earn bonus points for speed and accuracy.
